About Petal Runner

Petal Runner is a slice-of-life RPG, set in the futuristic city of Sapphire Valley.

An emerging fuel source derived from flowers — The "Leap Cell" — is powering a new generation of artificial pet technology.
Determined to be the best couriers of the new tech, Cali and Kira enroll in the Petal Runner program.

Gameplay

Charming environments!

Open world motorcycling!

Memorable Interactions!

Cast

CALI

“The final piece - and just in time too!”

Cali’s natural curiosity and inclination for mechanical work led them to build their very own motorcycle! As a lifelong resident of Starglow with a newfound sense of freedom, Cali enrolls in the Petal Runner delivery program and sets off to experience Sapphire Valley in their new role.

Home District: Starglow
Hobbies: DIY, Spending time with Kira, Daydreaming

KIRA

“Ooh Pineapple?! Gimme that sweet juice!!"

Kira is Cali’s lifelong AI companion. While most current-generation AI adhere well to the word of their owners, Kira is an older model with a rambunctious and loud spirit.

Home District: Starglow
Hobbies: Exploring, Spending time with Cali, Snacking

TIO

“Hello, [NAME_NOT_FOUND]! Welcome to Sparkle Parcel Co.!”

A friendly android with a few loose circuits, TIO is the receptionist for Sparkle Parcel Co. When they’re not busy directing customers, TIO enjoys lending a hand around the front office and delivery bay - often with mixed success.

Home District: Starglow
Hobbies: Organizing, Graphic Design

JO

“Going the extra mile only starts once you reach your destination!”

Head of Mentoring for the Petal Runner program’s Starglow branch, Jo is responsible for training all new recruits on delivery and installation of Leap Cells. Her warm demeanor makes her one of the friendliest Mentors across the entirety of Sapphire Valley.

Home District: Starglow
Hobbies: Music, Gaming

BENNY

“I hope your GAMING is better than your RACING!”

A fellow recruit in the Petal Runner program, Benny is Cali’s childhood friend and outspoken rival. Together with his noble AI companion Nubis, Benny is out to become top performer among the new recruits.

Home District: North Valley
Hobbies: Racing, General Antagonizing

OSCAR

“...”

Even though he’s gainfully employed at Sparkle Parcel Co, not much else is known about the reclusive and mysterious operator of Petal Runner dispatch.

Home District: Unknown
Hobbies: Anything coffee-related

DAHLIA

“Wow you’re certainly a peppy one, eh?!”

Dahlia is a cyber security major at North Valley High, and works part-time at Ping’s flower shop. She’s interested in the role of computerized control and diagnostic systems to monitor flower growth and lifecycle in the shop.

Home District: North Valley
Hobbies: Floriculture, Programming, Photography

PING

“…too often do we discard the broken; they are simply showing us their story.”

An aging model of android technology, Ping has changed gears in life and opened up his own flower shop within Starglow district.

Home District: Starglow (by way of North Valley)
Hobbies: Floriculture, Ornithology
